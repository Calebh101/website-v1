What platforms do my apps support?

  • Android: supported (since it's the easiest to build to on Flutter)
  • iOS: not supported (will be in the future)
  • macOS: unstable - macOS is a different story. Since I have an old iMac that I use to build my apps to Apple devices, it cannot utilize Metal, a required graphics engine for Flutter, thus giving me no way to test my apps on my Mac. Even though I could use a virtual machine, I just really don't feel like it. So, I will build to macOS, but it will not be tested at all, and will be unstable.
  • Linux: supported (since I develop on Linux)
  • Windows: not supported - I do not and have no plans of supporting Windows.
  • Web: not supported
